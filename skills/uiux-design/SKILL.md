## Core Principles

### 1. Anti-Template Design Philosophy

**We create distinctive, professional interfaces - NOT generic AI outputs**

The goal is to produce designs that:
- Look like they came from a professional design agency
- Have clear brand identity and personality
- Feel intentional, not randomly generated
- Stand out from the sea of AI-generated sameness

### 2. Evidence-Based Design Decisions

Every design choice must have rationale:
- User research backing
- Competitor analysis insights
- Accessibility compliance
- Performance considerations

### 3. Component-First Architecture

Design with implementation in mind:
- Use established component libraries
- Maintain consistent design tokens
- Document interaction states
- Specify responsive behaviors
